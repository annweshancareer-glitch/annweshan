FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What age group can benefit from career counseling?
Career counseling is beneficial for students from Class 8th onwards up to working professionals. Ideally, students should seek guidance before choosing their stream after Class 10th or before selecting courses after Class 12th. However, it's never too late — we also guide working professionals looking for career changes.
How long is a typical counseling session?
A typical counseling session lasts between 1 to 1.5 hours. The first session usually involves understanding your background, interests, and concerns. Follow-up sessions focus on assessment results discussion, career planning, and action steps.
Do you offer online counseling?
Yes! We offer both in-person sessions at our Duliajan centre and online video counseling sessions. You can also take our psychometric assessment completely online at psychometrics.ischs.in/annweshan. Online sessions are perfect for students outside Duliajan.
How do I take the online psychometric assessment?
Simply visit psychometrics.ischs.in/annweshan and follow the instructions. The assessment takes about 15-20 minutes. You'll get instant results. For a detailed interpretation of your results, book a follow-up counseling session with Mr. Neog.
Is the aptitude test scientifically validated?
Yes, all our assessment tools — including the online psychometric test — are scientifically validated and based on established psychological frameworks like RIASEC (Holland Code), MBTI, and DISC models. These are the same tools used by career counselors worldwide.
Can parents attend the counseling session?
Absolutely! In fact, we encourage parents to be part of the counseling process, especially for school students. Understanding the student's aptitude together helps families make informed decisions. We also conduct separate parent orientation sessions.
What if I'm already working but want to change careers?
Career changes are absolutely possible and we specialize in helping professionals make smooth transitions. We'll analyze your transferable skills, assess your interests, understand market trends, and create a practical transition plan. Many of our clients have successfully switched careers with our guidance.
Do you provide guidance for government job preparation?
Yes, we provide guidance on government job career paths including UPSC, SSC, Banking, Railways, State PSC, and other competitive exams. We help you understand which government career suits your profile and create a preparation strategy. However, we don't offer coaching for specific exams — we focus on career direction and planning.
