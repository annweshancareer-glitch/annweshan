Meet Your Career Guide

Mr. Deba Jyoti Neog

M.Sc., B.Ed. | Certified Career Counselor cum Coach

Mr. Deba Jyoti Neog is a passionate and dedicated career counselor from Duliajan, Assam, with over 12 years of experience in guiding students and professionals towards their ideal career paths. His journey in career counseling began with a simple belief — every student, regardless of their background or location, deserves the right career direction.

With his strong academic foundation in M.Sc. and B.Ed., combined with his certification as a Career Counselor cum Coach, Mr. Neog brings a unique blend of scientific assessment methodology and empathetic guidance. He specializes in helping students from small towns and rural areas of Assam and Northeast India achieve their big dreams.

Over the years, he has successfully guided more than 2,000 students in making informed career decisions, from stream selection after Class 10th to career transitions for working professionals. His approach combines scientifically validated psychometric assessments with personalized one-on-one counseling sessions.

Our Methodology

The Annweshan Approach

A proven 4-step methodology that has helped 2000+ students find their ideal career.

1

Assess

Start with our scientifically validated psychometric assessment to understand your aptitude, interests, and personality type.

2

Understand

Deep one-on-one discussion to understand your background, aspirations, family expectations, and personal strengths.

3

Guide

Create a personalized career roadmap with clear action steps, recommended courses, colleges, and timelines.

Support
